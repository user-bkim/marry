How to Add Your Photos

To add your own photos to this gallery:

  1. Create an images folder in your project
  2. Add your photos to the folder
  3. Replace the <div class="photo-placeholder"> sections with <img src="images/your-photo.jpg" alt="Description">

Example: <img src="images/family-portrait.jpg" alt="Family Portrait" class="gallery-photo">
